Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Mario Bueno ("I", "me", or "my") operates the ClaudioShort application ("App") and how I handle information in connection with the TikTok API.

1. Information I Collect

ClaudioShort accesses the following data through the TikTok API, solely to provide the App's functionality:

  • Basic profile information: TikTok username and user ID (required by the TikTok API to authenticate and perform actions on your behalf).
  • Video data: metadata of videos published through the App (title, description, upload status).
  • Video analytics: publicly available metrics of your videos (views, likes, comments, shares) for analysis purposes within the App.

I do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, payment information, location data, or any other personal data beyond what is strictly necessary to interact with the TikTok API.

2. How I Use Your Information

The data accessed is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account and act on your behalf (upload videos).
  • Display analytics and performance metrics of your content within the App.

I do not sell, rent, share, or disclose your data to any third party for marketing or commercial purposes.

3. Data Storage

ClaudioShort does not store your TikTok data on external servers. All API responses are processed locally or in memory and are not persisted beyond the active session unless explicitly required for App functionality (e.g., cached analytics).

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the TikTok API to interact with TikTok on your behalf. Your use of TikTok is governed by TikTok's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

5. GDPR – Rights of EU Users

If you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of access: you may request a copy of the data I hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: you may request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure: you may request deletion of your data at any time.
  • Right to restriction: you may request that I limit how I use your data.
  • Right to data portability: you may request your data in a portable format.
  • Right to object: you may object to the processing of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact me at mariobuenopen@gmail.com.

The legal basis for processing your data is your explicit consent given when you authorize the App via TikTok's OAuth flow.

6. Data Retention

I do not retain personal data beyond what is necessary to provide the App's features. You may revoke access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's settings, which immediately stops any further data access.

7. Children's Privacy

ClaudioShort is not directed to children under the age of 13. I do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data through this App, please contact me and I will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
